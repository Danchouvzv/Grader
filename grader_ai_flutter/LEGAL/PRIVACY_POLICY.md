# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-27

Grader.AI ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 1. Data We Collect
- **Account data**: name, email (when you sign in via Google/Apple)
- **Usage data**: practice sessions, scores, feature usage, app analytics
- **Audio data**: voice recordings and transcripts generated during IELTS practice sessions
- **Device data**: device model, OS version, app version, crash reports and diagnostics
- **Performance data**: IELTS band scores, improvement metrics, learning progress

## 2. How We Use Data
- **Core functionality**: Provide IELTS speaking practice and AI-powered feedback
- **Personalization**: Generate personalized learning insights and recommendations
- **Service improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and performance
- **Security**: Detect abuse, prevent fraud, and ensure service integrity
- **Communication**: Provide customer support and notify about app updates

## 3. Audio Data Processing
- **Recording**: We record your voice during IELTS speaking practice sessions
- **Transcription**: Audio is transcribed using Google Speech-to-Text API
- **AI Analysis**: Transcribed text is analyzed by OpenAI GPT-4 for IELTS scoring and feedback
- **Storage**: Audio recordings are stored securely in Firebase for your practice history
- **Retention**: Audio data is retained for the duration of your account or until you request deletion

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services that may process your data:
- **Firebase** (Google): Authentication, data storage, analytics
- **OpenAI**: AI analysis and feedback generation
- **Google Speech-to-Text**: Audio transcription
- **Apple App Store**: Payment processing for in-app purchases (when available)

## 5. Data Storage and Security
- **Location**: Data is stored in secure cloud services (Firebase, Google Cloud)
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access**: Only authorized personnel have access to user data
- **Retention**: Data is retained as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service

## 6. Data Sharing
- **No selling**: We do not sell personal data to third parties
- **Service providers**: We may share limited data with trusted service providers (analytics, crash reporting) strictly to operate the Service
- **Legal requirements**: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights
- **Consent**: We will ask for your explicit consent before sharing data for any other purpose

## 7. Subscriptions and Payments
- **Payment methods**: Premium subscriptions are available by request through our support channels
- **Payment processing**: When available, payments may be processed through Apple App Store In-App Purchases or direct payment methods
- **Payment data**: We do not store full payment details or credit card information
- **Subscription data**: We track subscription status for feature access in our secure database
- **Contact**: For subscription inquiries, contact support@grader.ai

## 8. Your Rights
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Portability**: Export your data in a machine-readable format
- **Withdrawal**: Withdraw consent where applicable
- **Contact**: For requests, email privacy@grader.ai

## 9. Children's Privacy
- **Age requirement**: The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or local age of consent)
- **Parental consent**: If you are under 18, please get parental consent before using the Service
- **Data deletion**: We will delete data of users discovered to be under 13
